Claydates - Create and be social!

What is Claydates?

Claydates is a social endeavour based around making things out of clay. An extremely therapeutic passtime we highly recommend you try with your friends, kids or even your enemies!

How to have a Claydate

It's really quite simple...

  1. Buy lots of clay
  2. Get hold of other participants
  3. Create and be social!

What should I make?

Whatever comes to mind!
